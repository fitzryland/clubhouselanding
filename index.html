What is Clubhouse?

Clubhouse is an online web application for managing shared housing. We help social people live together with ease and style. Manage bills, track chores, share messages, and find new housemates. We help people live better, by living together.

Why Clubhouse?

Like so many people, we choose to live in shared spaces. We like community, we like frugality, we like effective processes. Clubhouse is a tool for increasing the quality of life for us and people like us.

How can I join or try?

Clubhouse is in the process of being built. If you want to be an early beta tester, give us your name and email below and we will include you in the earliest versions.
